(About)

V Square Designs is a Nirmal-based interior design studio with a considerate and thoughtful approach. Our work is shaped by the city's rich craft heritage — from the famed Nirmal paintings and lacquerware to the earthy textures of local stone — and by how we respond to each client, each project, and the environment around it.

We demonstrate the beauty of simplicity, balance, and functionality through our work. Our team brings 10+ years of experience across residential, hospitality, and commercial projects spanning Telangana and Andhra Pradesh. We collaborate closely with local artisans, embedding Nirmal's cultural identity into every space we design.
